About Rusken Charitable Foundation

Reducing poverty by empowering Youth & Community through sustainability

Join Our Mission

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Rusken Charitable Foundation (RCF) supports community development through Education, Economic Empowerment, Agricultural Growth, Environmental Conservation, and Talent Development.

Our Objective

We empower rural youth by offering education, microfinance support, agricultural training, and environmental education, aiming to foster sustainable livelihoods and reduce poverty.

Core Principles

Vision

We envision a society where people work together to eliminate conditions that perpetuate misery and poverty, using education and collaboration as tools for change.

Mission

Initiating community development programmes to help disadvantaged people escape poverty and improve their quality of life.

Values

Innovation • Integrity • Sustainability • Collaboration • Empowerment

How We Operate

  • Making grants to individuals in need
  • Providing vocational training to communities
  • Offering microfinance loans to entrepreneurs
  • Supporting education through scholarships and materials

Our Development Approach

We believe in community-led development through active participation at every stage of our initiatives.

  • Identifying the areas of need
  • Identifying the necessary resources required to mitigate the needs
  • Identifying the means of raising the necessary resources
  • Raising the resources; properly utilizing them in a timely manner for target projects
  • Developing the methodologyand annual plans for implementing the interventions
  • Implementing the requisite strategies
  • Monitoring and evaluation of the programmes of the foundation
  • Effective leadership in partnership with the board of trustees
  • Partnering with the patron, board of trustees and other volunteers
  • Supporting the Board in the creation of a local and international network to publicise and popularise the opportunities in the foundation through a well maintained and constantly up-dated foundation website
  • Increasing awareness, nationally, of the foundation’s mission, vision, goals and ways to assist and become involved
  • Providing data and information for the foundation website and social media platforms to ensure robust engagement with the public and make the foundation more vibrant
